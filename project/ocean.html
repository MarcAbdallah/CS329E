  • About 13,000 tons of plastic are ingested by North Pacific fish yearly
  • There are an estimated 5.25 trillion pieces of plastic in our oceans
  • From 1950-1998, over 100 nuclear blast tests occurred in our oceans
  • 90% of the global ocean debris comes from 10 rivers alone
  • Coastal water contamination is responsible for 250 million clinical cases of human diseases annually
